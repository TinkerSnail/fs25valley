Katie (base-game **ANIMAL_DEALER** NPC). Full character from her dialogue tree
`~/fs25_npc_dialogue/npc/animalDealer/` (2026-06-25). Prioritized base-NPC cast
([[reference-basegame-npc-roster]]); the livestock mentor (her `help/animals/` cow tutorial) and half the
[[reference-basegame-npc-roster]] David↔Katie romance. STRUCTURAL FOIL to [[project-david-character]]:
she is ROOTED where Dave is uprooted.

**Who she is:** a 4th-generation animal farmer; grew up on the family farm; took it over **4 years ago**
so her parents could retire. Lifelong animal person — *"I've been working with animals all my life."*
Confident, breezy, worldly, hands-on, in control.

**Path (canon):** studied **HISTORY** in college (loves "the lessons you can learn from the past") →
changed major to **agricultural sciences**, minored in history (*"can't escape your family's legacy
sometimes"*) → after college **traveled the world working on farms** to learn other cultures' methods →
missed home, came back, took over from her mom + uncle. *"Hands-on person; flipping pages in textbooks
isn't enough for me."*

**Family (extends the world):** farm in the family **4 generations**; she + siblings grew up there.
- **Little brother** — in the city, **residency in family medicine** ("more about humans than animals";
  plans to come back). = the "respectable brother" David name-drops.
- **Little sister** — runs a **veterinary practice in the neighboring town**; Katie learned a lot from her.
  (doctor brother + vet sister = tidy symmetry; vet sister is a handy animal-health resource for stories.)
- **Parents** — handed off the farm; now **traveling for the first time in their lives** ("so happy for
  them"). Supportive family. Parental meet-cute: mom crashed into dad chasing a runaway wheel.

**Menagerie:** chickens, cows (incl. a named cow, **Rosy**), sheep, pigs, horses, bees + recent goats &
water buffaloes — *"an eclectic ensemble of creatures."*

**Voice:** confident, playful, a self-aware charmer (*"only because you're such a charmer!"*, *"you know I
love talking about myself"*); worldly story-spinner who trades in myths/tall tales (*"maybe I should start
a podcast"*); competent, amused, intellectually curious (history buff). Secure and teasing — the calm
counter to David's eager bumbling, which is WHY the romance works.

**Aspiration (her version of Dave's cake):** an **animal workshop / petting-and-learning place** — *"for
vacationers, school groups, anyone really"* who wants to meet the animals or learn to farm.

**Relationships:** David (romance — she warms to him, teases his klutziness, "Oh David, honey…"); Noah
(friendly ribbing); Walter (he brags about the player to her — *"your Grandpa was bragging"*); the player
(welcomes "Walter's grandkid," is the livestock/cow-care mentor).

**Use for us / theme:** Katie = the LEGACY/roots pole (*"can't escape your family's legacy… they all come
back one day"*) — rhymes directly with Walter's handoff + the player inheriting the farm. David =
reinvention; Katie = roots; the PLAYER sits between (inheriting like her, new like him). The competent
mentor who teaches animal care; the secure half of the romance.

David (base-game **FARMER** NPC; *"Call me Dave if you want to — I'm happy with either"*, canon). Full
character pulled from his dialogue tree `~/fs25_npc_dialogue/npc/farmer/` (2026-06-25). One of the
prioritized base-NPC cast ([[reference-basegame-npc-roster]]).

**Who he is:** a city transplant, ~2 years in Riverbend Springs, **no farming background** — a serial
searcher who finally found himself in the soil. Warm to a fault, comically inept in practice, quietly deep.

**Backstory (canon, in order):** studied **philosophy** (failed — "the city dude with his failed
philosophy studies") → chased **acting** (TV spots, theater; quit when he felt he was "doing something
that's not me for a living") → a trapped **office job** (good money, burned out, "money isn't everything")
→ got into farming **as a JOKE**: his buddy **Mike** gave him a farming VIDEO GAME as a gag; he played
"hundreds, if not thousands of hours through college," hooked his friends → balcony plants, then a garden
"together with my **ex-girlfriend**" → bought land through a friend, went for it. **"Now, I'm here. And
I'm happy. Tiny me wouldn't have imagined it."** ⇒ David is the AUDIENCE SURROGATE (played FS, then did it
for real) and the embodiment of second-chances / finding-purpose-late / money-isn't-everything.

**Voice:** effusive, instantly affectionate — "buddy," "friend," "neighbor," *"that doesn't mean I don't
love you. Already do."* Golden-retriever energy with a failed-philosopher's reflective streak (muses
unprompted: *"is farming art or a means to an end? Farmers are like painters, the landscape is the
canvas."*). Self-deprecating about his many mistakes (*"I'll tell you about all the mistakes I made,
hoping you don't hear about them from the others first"*).

**Relationships — and why he matters to US:**
- **Walter:** WALTER WELCOMED HIM. Lonely, disoriented, *"floated around not knowing where the handles
  are… and then your grandfather knocked on the door to check in. What a guy."* → David is LIVING PROOF
  of Walter's "welcome reprieve" ([[project-walter-story]]); deepens Walter by showing he does this for
  the whole town. David ≈ who the player might be WITHOUT the family farm/lineage.
- **Player:** peer + emotional MIRROR — both newcomers, but David came from nothing and CHOSE it. Shares
  his mistakes so you avoid them; *"let your grandpa be disappointed in ME instead of you."*
- **Katie:** smitten ([[reference-basegame-npc-roster]] David↔Katie romance). His self-sufficiency arc
  literally aims at *"baking a cake using only ingredients from my farm"* (his own wheat + his chickens'
  eggs) — i.e. the cake-for-Katie is HIS goal, grounded in his own farm (this is the honest version of
  the earlier "David's flour" overreach: he grows his own wheat, but canon never tied him to the bakery/
  flour mill).
- **Noah:** intimidated by the gruff stare, then *"it took me a while to realize he is way friendlier
  than he appears."*

**Use for us:** comic-relief-with-real-depth; the relatable-newbie foil to the player; concrete proof of
Walter's kindness; half of the cake romance; thematic echo of Walter (purpose, contribution, "risky but
freeing, and in the end rewarding"). A natural early friend the player makes.
